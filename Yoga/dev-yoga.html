Yoga & Meditation

FAQs

I’m a beginner—can I join?
Absolutely. We offer clear demonstrations and modifications for all bodies and fitness levels.
What results can I expect?
With regular practice, most people notice reduced stress, improved focus, better mobility, and deeper sleep within a few weeks.
Do I need to be flexible?
No—flexibility is a result, not a requirement. We focus on safe movement and breath.
What should I bring and how long are classes?
Wear comfortable clothing and bring a yoga mat and water (we have a few spares). Most classes are 60–90 minutes.
