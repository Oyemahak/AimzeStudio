FAQs

Is Nufree safe for sensitive skin?
Yes. Nufree is botanical and wax-free, so it’s gentler on skin and never sticks to the skin—reducing redness and irritation.
Which areas can be treated?
Nufree is safe for the entire body, including face, arms, legs, underarms, bikini, and more.
How is Nufree different from waxing?
Unlike wax, Nufree doesn’t harden or adhere to skin. It’s self-preserving antibacterial/antimicrobial and can be erased at any time, making treatments more comfortable.
Pre- and post-care tips
Before: arrive with clean, dry skin; avoid heavy lotions/oils; gently exfoliate 24 hours prior. After: keep the area clean, avoid heat/sun for 24 hours, and moisturize as recommended by your aesthetician.
