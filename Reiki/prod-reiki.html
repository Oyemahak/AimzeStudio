Reiki: Universal Life Energy Healing

What is Reiki?

Reiki (pronounced Ray-Key) is a Japanese word that means “universal life energy.” It refers to a gentle, holistic healing method that works by channeling this energy through the hands. Rediscovered by Dr. Mikao Usui in the early 20th century, Reiki supports the body’s natural ability to heal by restoring energetic balance. It works on all levels—physical, emotional, mental, and spiritual—to promote deep relaxation, reduce stress, and enhance overall well-being.

Reiki helps harmonize the body's Yin and Yang energies, gently releasing tension and restoring a sense of calm and clarity. It’s a natural, non-invasive way to support healing and personal growth.

Who Can Learn Reiki?

Anyone can learn Reiki. It doesn’t require any special background, belief system, or spiritual practice. Reiki is not affiliated with any religion—instead, it helps deepen your personal connection to yourself, your beliefs, and your loved ones.

No special lifestyle, meditation, or exercise routine is required. If you’re open to learning, Reiki is available to you.

How to Learn Reiki

Reiki is taught through a Master-Student relationship, guided by a certified Reiki Master or Teacher. Learning Reiki involves a series of attunements—special energetic initiations that enable you to channel Reiki energy for yourself and others.

This personal, hands-on teaching method makes Reiki a deeply transformative experience, often leading to lasting changes in well-being, perspective, and inner peace.
